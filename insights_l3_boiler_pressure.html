Boiler pressure threshold exceeded
Incident Active · 2h 14m Alpha Manufacturing Unit
Enabled
This insight is currently active
Resolution cannot be time-based. It must be triggered by a sensor value crossing the closing threshold, or manually acknowledged via a data input from an authorised user.
Description
Monitors the primary boiler steam pressure and triggers when pressure exceeds the safe operating range. This insight evaluates readings from pressure transducer PT-101 on a 30-second rolling average. Sustained high pressure can indicate a faulty safety valve, blocked steam line, or excessive heat input and must be resolved before returning the boiler to normal operation.
